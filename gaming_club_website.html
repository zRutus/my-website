Our Community Rules

  • Be respectful to all members
  • No swearing, racism, or hate speech
  • No spamming in chat channels
  • Keep discussions gaming-related and positive
  • Help new members feel welcome
  • Have fun and enjoy the games!

Quest & Tournament Rules

  • No cheating or exploiting game mechanics
  • Submit proof when required (screenshots, videos, etc.)
  • No alt accounts - one account per person only
  • Show up on time for tournaments and events
  • Follow the tournament brackets and match schedules
  • No ragebaiting or intentionally provoking other players
  • Complete quests within the given time frame
  • Report any issues or disputes to moderators
  • Fair play and good sportsmanship required
